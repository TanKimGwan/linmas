# Linmas 0.2.1

## Declarative policy packs

- Adds schema-versioned declarative JSON policy packs.
- Ships `baseline-appsec`, `cloud-change`, and `release-security` built-ins.
- Supports one explicitly named local policy file capped at 64 KiB.
- Evaluates only validated `ReviewResult` schema version 1 objects.
- Produces separate versioned `PolicyResult` output with rule-by-rule reasons.

## Safety boundary

Policy packs contain no executable code, commands, expressions, templates, plugins, provider hooks, or remote imports. They cannot modify findings, invoke providers, approve releases, accept risk, or remove human review. Safety and contract failures always produce `blocked`.

`pass` means only that the declared conditions were met. It does not prove security, compliance, certification, or approval. Human review remains required for every decision.
